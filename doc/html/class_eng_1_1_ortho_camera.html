Detailed Description

Implements a camera with an orthographic (parallel) projection.

In an OrthoCamera, objects maintain their size regardless of their distance from the viewer. The viewing volume is a simple rectangular cuboid, defined by its left, right, top, and bottom bounds, as well as the near and far clipping planes.
